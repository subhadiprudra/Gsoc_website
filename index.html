Why Gsoc Heat

As an Arabic proverb goes - "It is better to learn from the experienced than the learned". With this thought, the GNU Linux Users' Group and NITDgpOs presents GSoC Heat 4.0 where you can get an insight into the biggest forum of Open Source from the people who have aced it themselves. This begins with an analysis of the procedure to apply for GSoC and the requisites to boost your chances.
